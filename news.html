2026/2027 Enrollment Now Open — Apply Before 31st April

We are pleased to announce that applications for the upcoming academic year are officially open. Limited spaces remain across all classes — from Pre-Primary through to Grade 6. Early applicants receive priority placement and access to our Early Bird fee structure. Parents are encouraged to visit the school for a guided tour before submitting their applications. Download the application form from our website or collect one at the administration office.
