Why St. Teresa

An Education That Truly Matters

CBC-Aligned Curriculum

Fully aligned with Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, ensuring every child is prepared for future success.

Small Class Sizes

A 1:20 teacher-to-student ratio so every child receives personalised attention throughout their journey.

Holistic Development

Safe & Secure Campus

Fully fenced compound with CCTV and trained security — giving parents total peace of mind.

Digital Learning

Smart classrooms and e-learning resources that prepare students for a technology-driven world.

Feeding Programme

Nutritious, balanced meals provided daily — because learning always starts with a full stomach.
