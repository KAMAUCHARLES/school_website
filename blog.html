5 Ways to Support Your Child's Learning at Home

Research consistently shows that parental involvement is one of the strongest predictors of a child's academic success. But many parents wonder: what does meaningful involvement actually look like on a busy weekday evening? Here are five practical, research-backed habits our teachers recommend to every parent.

The first — and perhaps most powerful — habit is the "daily debrief": spending just 10 minutes each evening asking your child not what they learned, but what they found most surprising or interesting. This shifts the conversation from a performance review to genuine curiosity.
