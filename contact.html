FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the enrollment process? +
To enroll, fill in the contact form above or visit our administration office to collect an application form. You will need to submit the completed form, a copy of the child's birth certificate, last school report (if applicable), and a passport photo. An interview may be arranged.
What ages do you admit? +
We admit children from age 3 (PP1) through to age 12 (Grade 6). Our Special Education programme supports learners of all ages within this range. Contact us to discuss your child's specific needs.
Do you offer school transport? +
Yes, we have enough buses covering several Nyandarua and Laikipia estates. Routes and fees vary by location. Please contact the school office for current routes and availability in your area.
How are fees paid? +
Fees are payable at the beginning of each term via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or cheque. We offer payment plans for families who need them — please speak with our Finance Office. A sibling discount of 10% applies from the second child enrolled.
Can I book a school tour before enrolling? +
Absolutely — we encourage it! Tours run every Tuesday and Thursday at 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Fill in the contact form above, select "School Tour Request", and our team will confirm your booking within 24 hours.
Does the school follow the CBC curriculum? +
Yes. St. Teresa CLC is fully CBC-compliant and KICD-aligned. Our teachers are trained and certified in the Competency-Based Curriculum, and all assessments follow the MOE-approved framework. We also incorporate project-based learning and co-curricular activities.
