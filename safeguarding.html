Safeguarding Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Our Commitment

St. Teresa Children Learning Centre is committed to ensuring the safety and wellbeing of all children in our care. We follow strict safeguarding guidelines to protect children from harm.

2. Reporting Concerns

Any concerns regarding child safety should be reported immediately to the designated safeguarding officer at our school.

3. Staff Training

All staff undergo regular safeguarding training to recognize and respond to potential risks or abuse.

4. Safe Recruitment

Our hiring process includes thorough background checks to ensure that only suitable individuals are employed.

5. Child Protection Policies

  • Children are supervised at all times
  • Online activities are monitored
  • Procedures are in place to respond to incidents promptly

6. Parental Involvement

Parents and guardians are encouraged to engage with the school regarding safeguarding policies and concerns.

7. Contact

For questions about safeguarding, contact us at info@stteresaclc.ac.ke or call +254 700 000 000.
