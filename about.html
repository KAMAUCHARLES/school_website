About St. Teresa CLC

Discover our story, our values, and the dedicated team behind 4 years of exceptional education in Nyandarua and Laikipia.

Our Story

Nurturing Bright Minds Since 2022

St. Teresa Children Learning Centre was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate educators and parents who believed every child in Nyandarua and Laikipia deserved access to an outstanding, values-driven education — regardless of background.

What began as a small nursery school with 40 pupils and 3 teachers has grown into a thriving institution serving over 600 students, supported by more than 20 qualified staff across all disciplines.

We are fully registered with the Ministry of Education, CBC-certified, and KICD-aligned — and proud to consistently rank among Nyandarua's and Laikipia's top primary schools for academic outcomes and holistic development.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Excellence

We hold high standards for every student, teacher, and process — refusing to accept mediocrity in anything we do.

Care

Every child is known, loved, and supported. Our teachers invest deeply in the wellbeing of every learner.

Joy

We believe school should be a place children love coming to — where curiosity is celebrated and learning is joyful.

Inclusion

We welcome every child — regardless of ability, background, or learning style — and tailor support accordingly.

Integrity

We do what we say. Our promises to parents and students are always kept — transparency is non-negotiable.

Our Journey

4 Years of Growth

2022

Founded

St. Teresa CLC opens its doors with 40 pupils, 3 teachers, and a bold vision for quality early education in Nyandarua and Laikipia.

2023

Primary School Expansion

We expanded to offer full primary education through Grade 6, responding to overwhelming demand from our growing parent community.

2024

Special Education Programme Launched

Our dedicated Special Education unit opened, becoming one of the first primary schools in the region to offer full inclusion support.

2025

New Campus Building Completed

A state-of-the-art three-storey school block opened, featuring smart classrooms, a computer lab, an arts studio, and a library.

2025

CBC Certification & Digital Transformation

Full CBC accreditation achieved and smart technology installed in all classrooms, preparing students for the digital economy.

2025

600+ Students & Growing

St. Teresa CLC now serves over 600 students with 20+ staff and continues to expand co-curricular offerings and community outreach.
